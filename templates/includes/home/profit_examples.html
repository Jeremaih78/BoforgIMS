Profit Examples

Simple examples to show how daily sales add up.

Mug

Cost $1.20 -> Sell $5 -> Profit $3.80

10 mugs/day -> $38/day

T-Shirt

Cost $4 -> Sell $12 -> Profit $8

10 shirts/day -> $80/day

Cap

Cost $1.50 -> Sell $5 -> Profit $3.50

10 caps/day -> $35/day

Results vary; we teach pricing and sourcing.
